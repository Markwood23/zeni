Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zeni free to use?

Yes! Zeni is free to download and use. Core features like scanning, organizing, and basic editing are completely free. Premium features are available with a subscription.

Is my data safe with Zeni?

Absolutely. Your documents are stored locally on your device by default. We use encryption for all data transfers and never access your documents without your permission.

Can I use Zeni offline?

Yes! Most features work offline including scanning, organizing, and viewing documents. AI features and sharing require an internet connection.

How do I report a bug?

You can report bugs through the app at Profile → Help & Support → Report a Problem, or email us at support@zenigh.online.
